Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

AgentBib (“we”, “us”) is a macOS app for managing AI coding agents. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. In short: your code and your Claude sign-in never leave your Mac, and we collect only what we need to sell and support the app.

What we collect

  • At purchase — your email address (entered by you or via the payment), and payment status. Card details are handled entirely by our payment processor; we never see or store your card.
  • Device identifier — a one-way hash of your Mac’s hardware ID, used to bind your license to your device and prevent trial abuse. It is not personally identifiable and can’t be reversed to your hardware.
  • Basic app telemetry — the app version, your macOS version, and a “last seen” timestamp, sent when the app checks your license. Used to support you and know which versions are in use.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your source code, prompts, terminal output, or transcripts — all processed locally on your Mac.
  • Your Claude/Anthropic sign-in token — read only on your Mac to show your own usage; never transmitted to us.
  • No advertising identifiers, no third-party trackers, no selling of data.

How we use it

To issue and validate your license, deliver your license key, provide support, process refunds, and improve the app. That’s all.

Who we share it with

Only the service providers needed to run AgentBib: our payment processor (to take payment), our email provider (to send your license/receipts), and our cloud host (to run the license server). Each processes data solely to provide their service to us.

Data retention & your rights

We keep purchase and license records for as long as your license is active and as required for accounting/tax. You can request a copy of your data, or its deletion, by emailing us — we’ll respond within a reasonable time.
