Questions
Do I need an API key?

No. AgentBib securely reuses your existing Claude Code sign-in on your Mac to read your usage — there's nothing to paste, and your token never leaves your machine.

Is it a subscription?

Never. It's a one-time payment for a lifetime license, and all future updates are included. Buy once, own it.

How many Macs can I use it on?

Solo = 1, Duo = 2, Team = 5. You can move your license between Macs anytime — activate on a new one, deactivate an old one to free the slot.

Does it work with agents other than Claude Code?

Yes — Claude Code today, with Codex and other agents detected automatically and shown with their own color. More on the way.

Is my data private?

Everything runs locally on your Mac. The local server is loopback-only by default (not exposed to your network unless you opt in for phone access), and your sign-in token is only ever read to show your own usage.

What do I need to run it?

A Mac on macOS 14 or later — Apple Silicon or Intel. The download is notarized by Apple, so it opens cleanly.
